# ASCENFORM Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

## Overview
ASCENFORM ("we", "us") is a climbing form analysis app. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data We Collect
- Videos you upload for analysis
- Video frames extracted for AI analysis
- AI-generated coaching feedback and analysis results
- Your feedback on analysis quality (helpful/not helpful)

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide climbing form analysis and coaching feedback
- To improve our AI analysis accuracy over time
- We do not sell your data to third parties

## Third-Party Services
- We use Anthropic Claude API to analyze climbing video frames
- Video frames are sent to Claude API for analysis

## Data Storage
- Analysis results and feedback may be stored to improve our service
- You may request deletion of your data by contacting us
